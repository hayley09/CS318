About CFSA

The Chinese Friendship and Scholarship Association (CFSA) is a non-profit organization affiliated with the International Education Office of the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire whose aims are:

  • To facilitate communication, friendship, and fellowship among CFSA members.
  • To promote understanding and appreciation of Chinese culture and history to people in the north central region of Wisconsin.
  • To assist new Chines faculties, staff, and visiting scholars during their settle-in periods in Wisconsin.

What does CFSA do?

Here are some examples...
  • Celebration of Chinese festivals (Lunar new year, moon festivals, dragon etc.)
  • Common interest groups (Tai chi, sewing, ...)
  • Chinese language classes
  • Seminars/lectures on Chinese culture and history
  • Public presentation of Chinese movies, arts, music...
  • Provision of educational and cultural resources (library, museum..)
  • Sponsoring community/scholarly cultural projects
